Water, Cold

Ice cold water, perfect for any human person

Ingredients

  • 1 Glass
  • 1 Refrigerator
  • 8oz of Water
  • Thirst

Directions

  1. Gather 8oz of clean drinking water
  2. Add 8oz of water to a drinking glass
  3. Place glass of water into refrigerator for no less than 45 minutes
  4. Drink!
